Foundation of Simple and Compound Interest:

______________________
                      |
Simple Interest (SI)  |
______________________|

Simple Interest is the interest calculated only on the 
original principal amount for the entire time period.

👉 Interest does not change every year — it stays constant.

Formula:
				P x R x T
		SI = ---------------
                   100
				   
Meaning of Symbols:
* P = Principal (starting money)
* R = Rate of interest (per year%)
* T = Time (in years)
* SI = Simple Interest

Total Amount:
		
		A = P + SI
		
Example:
Q) Find SI on Rs 1000 at 10% for 2 years

			      1000 x 10 x 2
			SI = ---------------- =  200
				        100 
						
Interest = Rs 200
Total Amount = Rs 1200	

________________________
                        |
Compound Interest (CI)  |
________________________|

Compound Interest is the interest calculate on 
principa + previously earned interest.

Interest keeps increasing every year (interest on interest)

                      T
	A = P (1 +  R/100)
	
Meaning of symbols:
* A = Final Amount
* P = Principal 
* R = Rate (%)
* T = Time (years)

  CI = A - P

Example:
Q) Find CI on Rs 1000 at 10% for 2 years.

Solution:

	A = 1000 (1 + 10/100)²
	

    A = 1000 (1 + 0.10)²
		  
	A = 1000 (1.10)²

	A = 1000 x 1.10 x 1.10
	
	A = 1000 x 1.21

	A =  1210
	
Interest = Rs 210
Total Amount = Rs 1210

numbers is/are:
A. 3  <--
B. 1
C. 2
D. 4

Solution:

Let the two numbers be:

23𝑥 and 23y (since HCF = 23)

Step 1: Form equation from sum

	23𝑥 + 23y = 161
	23(𝑥 + y) = 161
	𝑥 + y = 161/23
	𝑥 + y = 7
	
	
Step 2: Find pairs of 𝑥,y such that:

	𝑥 + y = 7
	HCF of 𝑥 and y = 1 (because 23 is the only common factor)

	1 + 6 = 7
	2 + 5 = 7
	3 + 4 = 7

	Possible pairs:

	(1, 6) → HCF = 1 
	(2, 5) → HCF = 1 
	(3, 4) → HCF = 1 

Step 3: Convert back to actual numbers
	(23, 138)
	(46, 115)
	(69, 92)

All are valid.

Final Answer:

 Therefore, Total possible pairs = 3
